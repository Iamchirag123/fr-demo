    ABOUT THE PROJECT

    Orchid Sky is premium residential project jointly developed by HN Safal, Goyal & Co and Gala Infra. Project is situated at Club O7 Road, Shela, Ahmedabad.

    Orchid Sky offers commercial shops on Ground and First Floor, which enjoy a strategic location advantage. These will fulfil all the necessities of the residents of the project and the surrounding locality.

    Orchid Sky has 664 units of 3 BHK & 2 units of 2 BHK. 3 BHK RERA Carpet Area range from 810-815 Sq.ft., For 2 BHK RERA Carpet Area is 656 Sq.ft. The Project has 75 shops on Ground Floor and 34 shops on First Floor.

    Orchid Sky has been created and designed to offer a holistic and enriching living experience for its residents. Our objective is to introduce our residents to ‘A New Way of Living’.

    Through Orchid Sky, we wish to redefine what ‘affordable’ means and raise the bar for quality for all such projects.
